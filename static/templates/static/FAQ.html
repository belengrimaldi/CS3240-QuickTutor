FAQ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register?

Account registration at QuickTutor is required if you will be receiving or giving tutoring. You can register by logging in through any valid Google account at the home page!

How can I apply to be a tutor?

You can become a tutor by contacting us and demonstrating your knowledge/classes taken on the subject you want to offer your services.

How do tutors get paid?

All payments will be processed by our system online. We will accept payments made through the Stripe API (in progress) and are working on venmo integration.

How do I begin?

Users will be able to create an account on QuickTutor and be listed as a tutor, tutee, or both. Start by updating your profile and filling out your bio, classes taken, and classes you need help in. Then tutees will be able to request tutors through either the list view, or by locating them on the map.

What if I'm not satisfied with my tutoring?

Each user profile will have a rating system for how efficient they were as a tutor (if applicable). Please review this before making a request to be tutored by a specific individual. We will also be monitoring the system and having penalties and possible bans for tutors who are not doing their job properly. For further help, please contact us at support@quicktutor.com.

Is it safe?

Yes. No private data about any users will be necessary or allowed when using our application. Meeting places will all be at public university study areas, and meeting times will only take place during normal business hours. If you have any further concerns please contact us at support@quicktutor.com.
